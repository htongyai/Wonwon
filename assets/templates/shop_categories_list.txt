SHOP CATEGORIES LIST
====================

Use these exact category names in your Excel import file:

1. clothing
2. footwear
3. watch
4. bag
5. electronics
6. appliance

MULTIPLE CATEGORIES:
If a shop offers multiple categories, separate them with commas:
Example: clothing,footwear,bag

NOTES:
- Use lowercase only
- No spaces before or after commas
- These are the exact category IDs used in the system
- The system will automatically match these to the proper category names and icons

CATEGORY DESCRIPTIONS:
- clothing: Clothing repair services
- footwear: Shoe and footwear repair  
- watch: Watch repair services
- bag: Bag repair services
- electronics: Computer, phone and electronic device repair
- appliance: Electrical appliance repair
